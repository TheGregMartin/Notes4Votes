Moving to the 2nd or 3rd round

Discard all Hits tokens used in the scoring of the a cappella groups. (Players do not discard their own Hits tokens!)

Discard all claimed Share tokens. Discard the Venue card and all five Judge cards. Discard all Unexpected Change cards that were played during the round just completed.

All players may discard any Unexpected Change card(s) they wish to. Then, all players draw from the Unexpected Change deck until they are holding two cards.

Discard the a cappella groups (A and B cards) who did not place 1st or 2nd in the round just completed. Move the 1st place group to the bottom row (to become Group F) and the 2nd place group to the row just above it (to become Group E).

Construct the small stacks of face-up Content tokens for each of the six groups (A–F), using the three tokens for the appropriate Round (2 or 3); the top Content token should be the one with the highest 1st-place payoff (10 or 16, respectively), and the bottom Content token should be the one with the lowest 1st-place payoff (6 and 8, respectively).

Deal a new Venue card face up into its spot, and place the Round 2 or Round 3 token (as appropriate) to its left.

Deal four new a cappella groups (from the A and B decks) into the top four rows, to become Groups A–D.

Deal five new Judge cards: one new judge face up into the leftmost judge spot, and four more judges face down to its right.

The player to the left of the Cutoff token turns it over to its Downbeat side and takes the first turn in the next competition round.
