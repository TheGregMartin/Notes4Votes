Player actions

On a player’s turn, they must take a single action chosen from the following list.

Publish content. The player discards R of their Hits, where R is the current round number (1, 2, or 3). Then, they claim the topmost Content token in any a cappella group’s stack.

  • This action is available as long as there is at least one unclaimed Content token.
  • A player must have enough Hits to discard to take this action.
  • A player may possess any number of Content tokens from any combination of groups, including multiple Content tokens for the same group.

Share content. The player chooses a Content token that has been claimed by another player and does not have a Share token on it. They give R Hits, where R is the current round number, to the player possessing the chosen Content token. Then, they claim a matching pair of Share tokens, keep the larger one, and place the smaller one on the Content token.

  • This action is available as long as there is still at least one pair of unused Share tokens.
  • A player must have enough Hits to give to take this action.
  • A player may only take this action once per competition round.

Reveal an unexpected change. The player chooses an a cappella group that does not yet have an Unexpected Change card next to it, and plays an Unexpected Change card from their hand face up to the left of that group (abiding by any restrictions on the card).

  • An a cappella group can never have more than one Unexpected Change card assigned to it.
  • Players do not gain or discard Unexpected Change cards during competition rounds—they may do so at the ends of Rounds 1 and 2.

Post a judge’s bio. The player claims 1 Hit from the general supply. Then, they turn over the leftmost face-down Judge card, which stays face up for the rest of this round. If the last (rightmost) Judge card was the one just turned over, the player claims the Downbeat token, turns it over to its Cutoff side, and places it to their left.

  • This action is available as long as there is still at least one face-down Judge card.

Run an ad. (This action is only available if there are no remaining face-down Judge cards.) The player claims 1 Hit from the general supply.
