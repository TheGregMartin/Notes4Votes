Group attributes and Hits

There are five attributes that judges might be looking for during competitions: upper voices, lower voices, vocal percussion, arrangements, and choreography. Each a cappella group (comprised of one A card and one B card) will be strong in some of these attributes but weak in others. The list below shows the colour, letter, and symbol representing each of these attributes.

Hits are a measure of your media success (the “victory points” of the game). Each Hit is represented by an eighth note. Longer notes represent multiple hits according to their musical duration—see the table below for specifics. (Hits will also be used to keep track of the judges’ scores for a cappella groups in the competition rounds.)
