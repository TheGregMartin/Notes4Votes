Setting up the game

Shuffle the Venue deck. Deal one Venue card face up in the top left corner of the play area. Put the Venue deck aside (it will be used a while later). Put the Round 1 token to the left of the face-up Venue card.

Place the six Group labels (A through F) in a vertical line below the Venue card. In a small stack to the right of each Group label, put the three Round 1 Content tokens corresponding to the Group, in order from highest 1st place payoff (6) on the top to lowest (3) on the bottom.

Shuffle A deck and B deck. To the left of each Group label, deal an A card and a B card face to form an a cappella group. Put the A and B decks aside (they will be used a while later).

Shuffle the Judge deck. Deal one Judge card face up to the right of the Venue card; then deal four more Judge cards face down in a row extending to the right. Put the Judge deck aside (it will be used a while later).

Place the Performance dice in a pile in the middle of the table (to be used later).

Place the Hits tokens (eighth notes, quarter notes, half notes, whole notes) where players can reach them. Give each player 8 Hits (suggested: 1 half note, 1 quarter note, 2 eighth notes).

Place the seven pairs of Share tokens near the Hits tokens.

Shuffle Unexpected Change deck. Deal two Unexpected Change cards face down to each player (they can look at their own). Put the remaining deck where players can reach it.

Give the Downbeat token to the player who has most recently been on a stage. They will take the first action of the game.
